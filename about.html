--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## ABOUT ME

I am a Senior R&D Engineer of Cognitive Computing Team (previously part of Artificial Intelligence Department) from Ant Group and executive member of Wuhan University - Ant Group United Laboratory. With over 7 years' academia and industry experience in digital signal processing, computer vision and multimodal understanding, I have participated in developing several award-winning tech products (e.g. 定损宝, 亿亩田), achieving well-balanced research and commercial accomplishment. I received my Bachelor degree from Zhejiang University and graduated with honors. Afterwards, I continued my Master education with Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne (EPFL), majoring in Communication System in Computer and Communication Department. During my stay in Europe, I both worked in multinational corporation's research institute (e.g. Sony European Technology Center) and cutting-edge start-ups (e.g. nViso SA and Tractable Ltd.). Recently, I'm fascinated in building Artificial General Intelligence and working towards its potential applications in various domains.
