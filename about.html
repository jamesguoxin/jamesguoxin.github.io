--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About Me

I am a Principal Research Scientist in AI at the Shanghai Academy of Artificial Intelligence for Science (SAIS). My work focuses on AI for Life Science, especially multiomics and multimodal foundation models and their clinical applications. This role builds on my previous experience in computer vision, multimodal perception, and vision foundation models.

Before joining SAIS, I spent 8+ years at Ant & Alibaba Group conducting applied AI research. My work there contributed to award-winning systems such as 定损宝 and 亿亩田. I was also an executive member of the Wuhan University - Ant Group United Laboratory, where I collaborated with researchers and engineers on SkySense, a foundation model for remote sensing imagery.

I hold a Master's degree in Communication Systems from EPFL and a Bachelor's degree with honors from Zhejiang University. I have also worked in Europe with Sony and Tractable.

My research interests span multiomics foundation models, AI for biology, computer vision, and multimodal perception. I have published 15+ papers, served as a reviewer for major AI conferences and journals, and hold 10+ international patents issued across Europe, the United States, Japan, Korea, Singapore, and other jurisdictions.
