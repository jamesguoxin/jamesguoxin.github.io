--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## ABOUT ME

I am a Senior R&D Engineer of Cognitive Computing Team from Ant Group. With over 7 years academia and industry experience in digital signal processing, computer vision and multimodal understanding, I have participated in developing several award-winning tech products (e.g. 亿亩田), gaining well-balanced research and commercial success. I received my Bachelor degree from Zhejiang University and graduated with honors. Afterwards, I continued my Master education with Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne (EPFL in French), majoring in Communication System in Computer and Communication Department. During my stay in Europe, I both worked in multinational coorperation's research institute (i.e. Sony European Technology Center) and cutting-edge start-ups (i.e. nViso SA and Tractable Ltd.). Recently, I'm facinated in building Artificial General Intelligence and working towards its potential applications.
